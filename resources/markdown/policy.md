# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-22

### 1. Overview
tesher harmony respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices regarding that data.

- Contact & account data: name, email, phone, billing/shipping address.

- Communications: messages you send us, customer support interactions, marketing preferences.

- To communicate about orders, account updates, and support requests.

- To improve our services, analyze usage, and prevent fraud.

### 4. Sharing and Disclosure
- Service providers: shipping, payment processors, analytics — only as needed to perform services.

### 5. Cookies & Tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies for session management, analytics, and advertising. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

### 6. Security
We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. No system is completely secure; we will notify you of breaches as required by law.

### 7. Data Retention
We retain personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

### 8. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to or restrict processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

### 9. Children
Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from young children.
